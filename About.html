About Us

Philosophy, Mission and Vision

Philosophy MEGABYTE COLLEGE Foundation, Inc. shall provide a quality education that will foster quality of life for the GLORY OF GOD. Vision To produce God-fearing, responsible, productive, and globally competitive graduates to become future leaders that will make a difference. Mission Develop students with quality and excellent education binding to the highest standard of COMPETENCE, HONESTY, INTEGRITY, LOVE ,AND DEDICATION (CHILD).

Brief History

A dream of providing computer education of the founder, CRESENCIO B. BUENARTE became a reality when Megabyte College came into the existence in 1985. Its main purpose is to be of service to Computer Education to meet the growing needs of computer literates in our province most especially in the 2nd District of Pampanga. From its humble beginning, it was named MEGABYTE COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY RESOURCE CENTER, that offered Computer short term courses as BASIC PROGRAMMING, ASSEMBLER, FORTRAN, DATABASE, WORD STAR, MULTIPLAN AND vocational courses TYPEWRITING and STENOGRAPHY. In 1994, MEGABYTE COLLEGE TECHNOLOGY RESOURCE CENTER was changed into MEGABYTE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLY FOUNDATION Inc. to further the strengthen its services and curricular offerings. Additional programs were offered: Two year COMPUTER SECRETARIAL, Two year ASSOCIATE IN COMPUTER SCIENCE. It was 1997 when MEGABYTE COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY was renamed. It was able to acquire ownership of its present site in Sto. Cristo, Pampanga and in Mabical Floridablanca, Pampanga and started putting up its own building. The modern concrete buildings include administrative, teaching, sports and recreational facilities. Thus MEGABYTE COLLEGE environment becomes more conducive to learning. With the noble aim of making the school more responsive to the changing needs of the community, the administration with the diligent effort and guidance of DR. FILIPINAS A. BUENARTE continues to its growth and success. The school seeks to lead students gain world class competence in the area they can best utilize their human talents and resources through a well planned curricular and co-curricular programs, effective and efficient instruction, quality support services and functional research. At the moment, the school offers the following courses, Pre-Elementary (Kinder I & Kinder 2, Grade School (Grade 1 to Grade 6), Junior High School (Grade 7-Grade 10), Senior High School (Grade 11-Grade 12), and College Courses, Bachelor in Elementary Education (BBED), Bachelor in Secondary Education (BSED), Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS), Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCRIM) In 2019, on its 35th year of providing QUALITY EDUCATION FOR THE GLORY OF GOD, MEGABYTE COLLEGE was named MEGABYTE COLLEGE FOUNDATION INC. It proves its worth by the success of its graduates who passed the board examinations and leads job after graduation. Through its humble beginnings and its name, it continues to build RESPONSIBLE LEADERS AND ACHIEVERS.
