Basic Education

Step by Step Admission Procedure for Basic Education Department

Step 1 Principal's Office Evaluation Information Dissemination Tuition Discount Scholarship Step2 Guidance Office Interview Recommendation Step 3 Registrar's Office Fill up the Registration Form Attached: Form 138 NSO / PSA Step 4 Principal’s Office Assessment: Discount / Scholarship Step 5 Cashier’s Office For Payment Note: Cashier will forward to the Registrar’s copy of enrolment form

Admission/Enrollment Procedures for Elementary

Pre-Elementary 1. Interview and Orientation (Head Teacher) 2. Accompaniment by Guardian/Parent 3. Birth Certificate – NSO or PSA 4. 2 copies of latest 1x1 colored pictures Elementary A. Grade 1 1. Taking of Qualifying Exam (Reading) 2. Submission of F138, Checklist (Pre-Elementary) 3. Copy of Learner’s Reference Number (LRN) 4. 2 copies of latest 1x1 colored pictures 5. Birth certificate – NSO or PSA 6. Interview and Orientation (Head Teacher/Principal) 7. Accompaniment by Guardian/Parent B. Transferees 1. Submission of F138 (card) with LRN 2. 2 copies of the latest 1x1 colored pictures 3. Birth Certificate – NSO or PSA 4. Interview and Orientation by the Head Teacher 5. Accompaniment by Guardian/Parent C. Old Students 1. Submission of F138 (card) 2. Accompaniment by Guardian/Parent

Admission/Enrollment Procedures for High School

A. Requirements for Incoming Grade 7 students: 1. Form 138 or Report Card with Learner’s Reference Number (LRN) 2. Birth Certificate – NSO or PSA 3. 2 copies of 1x1 colored pictures 4. Certificate of Good Moral Character B. Old Students 1. Submission of F138 (card) C. Transferees 1. Must have passed the interview conducted by the Guidance Counselor 2. Form 138 with Learner’s Reference Number (LRN) 3. Certificate of Good Moral 4. Birth Certificate – NSO or PSA
