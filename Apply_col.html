Requirements for Enrollment For Freshmen:

Form 138 / Form 137 Certificate of Good Moral Character 2 pcs. 2x2 picture with white background Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate 2 pcs. Long Brown Envelope

For Transferee

For Transferee: Transcript of Records and Eligibility to Transfer / Dismissal Certificate of Good Moral Character 2 pcs. 2x2 picture with white background Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate 2 pcs. Long Brown Envelope

Enrollment Procedures for Old Students

Step 1 Present your Clearance and Evaluation form / Assessment form at the registrar's office. Step 2 Get the Registration Form and Fill-up the form Step 3 Go to the Registrar's office for evaluation of subjects and for the schedule to be enrolled Step 4 Go to the cashier and pay the enrollment fee. Step 5 Go to Business Office for the issuance of Class Card
